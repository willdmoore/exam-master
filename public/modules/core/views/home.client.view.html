This computer-based study program was developed to assist individuals in preparation for their pre-licensing education training for Pennsylvania's Property and Casualty Producer Examination. It is the companion software of the study manual. Essentials of Property and Casualty Insurance.

Sign In! Get Started!

It is recommended that you review your study manual in depth prior to using EXAM MASTER Interactive.

A complete overview including an audio summary of this Pre-Licensing Education Course is found in the introduction.

The Study Materials page a collection of sections for studying and previewing all relevant materials. It includes a Chapter Preview section, which contains over 450 slides. The Chapter Review feature includes open-ended questions that you should be able to answer after studying a chapter module. The Flash Cards section features over 500 key terms and concepts in a flash card format. This will help students learn new terms and concepts.
